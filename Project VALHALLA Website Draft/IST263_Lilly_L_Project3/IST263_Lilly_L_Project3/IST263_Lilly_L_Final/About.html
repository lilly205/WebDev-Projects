About Us

Throughout the years, I've personally always been frustrated when I do everything I possibly can but my teammates just simply are not on the same page as me. That's when it hit me. Players need a forum that they can find similarly skilled players to play with. But why stop there? Everyone should be able to find a teammate to play with regardless if they want to play competitively or socially. Thus Project VALHALLA was born. I know some higher skilled players may doubt the legitimacy of this website because "Oh he doesn't know what a competitive player would want". But as you can tell from my stats in games like Halo and Battlefield, I know a thing or two about what a competitive player wants in a teammate.

I hope that every player can find this site as a hub for finding great teammates and even better friends. Happy hunting. ~ Levi
